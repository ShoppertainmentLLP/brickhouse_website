At 27 Brick House, we believe fitness is not simply about looking better — it is about living better. Being fit means having the strength to carry your groceries with ease, the energy to keep up with your children, the confidence to move without limitation, and the resilience to enjoy an active, fulfilling life for years to come.

Every member arrives with a different goal. For some, it may be building strength and muscle. For others, it may be improving athletic performance, losing body fat, moving pain-free, or simply feeling healthier and more energetic. Whatever your starting point, our role is to provide the structure, coaching, accountability and environment needed to help you succeed.

True fitness extends beyond strength and endurance. It includes mobility, stability, balance, coordination, recovery and the ability to perform well in everyday life. That is why our coaching approach focuses on developing complete human performance rather than isolated fitness qualities.

Through personalised programming, expert guidance, progress tracking and a supportive community, we give you the tools to achieve meaningful, lasting results.

All we ask is that you show up consistently. We’ll take care of the rest.

How We Train

Four programmes. One goal.

Every programme can be adapted to your current ability and goals — whether you’re completely new to training or have years of experience.

Hustle

60 Min Full Body Strength

HUSTLE is our foundational strength training programme for individuals who want to become stronger, move better and build a resilient body.

Built around full-body sessions, it combines strength development, movement quality, mobility and conditioning to create sustainable long-term progress.

Best for
  • Building strength
  • Improving body composition
  • Developing movement quality
  • Enhancing overall fitness
  • A strong foundation for life

Play

60 Min Performance Training

PLAY is our athletic development programme designed to improve how your body moves, performs and responds to physical challenges.

Sessions focus on speed, power, coordination, agility, balance, mobility and injury resilience — qualities that translate directly into sport and everyday life.

Best for
  • Recreational athletes
  • Competitive athletes
  • Injury prevention
  • Speed & agility development
  • Improving athletic performance

Grind

90 Min Hypertrophy Training

GRIND is our dedicated muscle-building programme, designed around proven hypertrophy principles.

It uses progressive overload, varied stimuli, tempo manipulation, supersets, mechanical drop sets and advanced bodybuilding techniques to maximise muscle growth and physique development.

Best for
  • Building muscle mass
  • Improving physique
  • Body recomposition
  • Increasing training volume
  • Mind-muscle connection

Sweat

40 Min Conditioning & Work Capacity

SWEAT is our conditioning-focused programme designed to improve cardiovascular fitness, endurance, work capacity and overall health.

Using rowing, SkiErg, carries, sled work, bodyweight movements and metabolic circuits, it challenges your energy systems while keeping training engaging and effective.

Best for
  • Fat loss
  • Cardiovascular fitness
  • Building endurance
  • Increasing daily energy
  • Overall health & conditioning
Where You Train

The Facility

27 Brick House was designed to be more than a gym — it is a training environment built for people who value their health, performance and quality of life.

From busy professionals and entrepreneurs to athletes, fitness enthusiasts and active retirees, our facility caters to people who want to get more out of life. Every square foot has been thoughtfully designed to support better movement, better training and better results.

Our multi-level facility combines strength, hypertrophy, conditioning, mobility, recovery and performance development under one roof. Whatever your goal — build muscle, lose body fat, improve performance, move pain-free, or simply stay strong as you age — you’ll find the tools and guidance to get there.

We believe great coaching deserves great equipment. We’ve carefully curated a collection of world-class training equipment, selecting each piece based on biomechanics, functionality, versatility and training effectiveness. Every station serves a purpose.

The best training facility isn’t defined by the machines inside it. It’s defined by the results achieved by the people who train there.
